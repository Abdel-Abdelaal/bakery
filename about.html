About Midnight Bakehouse

We are a tiny collective of pastry nerds obsessed with playing with nocturnal flavors and tiny, golden-baked friends. Each menu drop is inspired by city nightscapes and is meant to travel warm, ready for a midnight picnic or early-morning coffee run.

Our sweets are made with direct-trade cacao, local honey, and a generous pinch of delight. The studio kitchen runs on analog timers, thoughtful playlists, and serious sprinkles.

What makes us different

  • Small-batch, limited releases every Friday night
  • Zero artificial colors; color comes from beet reductions and cocoa
  • Neighborhood delivery using bikes and curiosity
